Welcome to UniKaksha India’s leading online digital educational platform that makes you industry-ready through hands-on learning delivered by our exceptionally qualified mentors. Whether you’re a fresh graduate, a working professional, an enterprise or a business, we have a valuable range of industry-vetted programs in Data Science, Machine Learning, Digital Marketing, Programming and many other trending technologies.

Who we are

In the digital age, there are a number of online digital education platforms available. Then there’s UniKaksha. We are not the oldest. We are not the biggest. But we are DRIVEN.


The majority of our efforts are motivated by the objective of giving every student the chance to pursue the career they desire, regardless of their background or education. By combining India’s top mentors, an industry-relevant curriculum, unparalleled placement assistance and an extremely supportive environment, we are committed to helping individuals upskill themselves and excel in this rapidly changing technological landscape. All our certification programs are reviewed and updated regularly by senior practitioners from industry, business, and academia.
